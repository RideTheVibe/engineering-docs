The frame is made out of 14 Gauge mild steel tubing, using Tube Bending, Tube Coping and MIG welding manufacturing processes. Here is an annotated visual for better clarity of the names:
<ImageProvider lightImage={frameRenderAnnotated} darkImage={frameRenderAnnotated} height={800}/>

The frame is composed of the following subcomponents:

- Seat Stay
- Seat Cage
- Ladder Supports
- Handle Bar Stem
- Dropouts

The CAD software used to design the frame is Fusion360 and the drawing files will be shared through AutoDesk 360 links below. Backup PDFs are also available in the Metal section of the [All Downloadables](/docs/AllDownloadables#metal) page.
